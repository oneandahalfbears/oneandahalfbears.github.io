About Me

My name is Julian Vitto, and I am an aspiring filmmaker currently working on my bachelor’s in film at SAE.

I have a deep passion for storytelling and film, with a drive to bring stories to life through my art. Ever since I was a child, I knew that I needed to work in film as it is my main motivator in life and it brings me joy, and my cultivation of this passion has only made my love for film grow stronger over the years.

I pride myself on my creative approach and my desire to learn and grow and showcase strong themes and skills through the work I put out. I’ve worked on many projects both in and out of university.
